Starter Kit

What is this?

This is a blank Starter Kit for you to jump start your project.

It contains QuillPro's basic layout so you can populate it with your awesomeness!

How do I use this Starter Kit?

  1. Simple! Just open up your root folder and locate starter-kit-rtl.html
  2. Copy it into your project folder
  3. Rename it from starter-kit-rtl.html to index-rtl.html (or whatever extension you require for your project)
  4. Open your favorite text editor and start building your awesome app!
  5. That's it!
